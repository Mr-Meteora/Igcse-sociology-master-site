💡 Tips

how to not lose free marks

Learn Your Sociologists

Examiners absolutely love it when you name-drop theorists correctly. “A sociologist said society is bad” is not what you can simply write. “Durkheim argued that…” is.

PEEL Paragraphs

Point → Evidence → Explanation → Link back to the question. This formula is your GOD.

Definition Questions

Short, precise, with an example. The examiner doesn't want an essay.

Mark Your Own Papers

With the mark scheme open. It's the fastest way to learn how examiners think and where you're losing marks.

Time Management

Practice past papers under timed conditions, get a smooth pen, and train your hand.

Sociological Terms

Use and learn specific sociological vocabulary that shows you know what you're talking about.

🧠 Advice

aka “what actually works”

Active Recall

Highlighting your notes in four different colours and calling it “studying” is not studying. Test yourself. Close the book. Say it out loud.

Spaced Repetition

Apps like Anki are built for this. Review things just before you'd forget them instead of cramming.

Past Papers From Day One

The exam has a format. Get familiar with it early so it doesn't surprise you.

Teach Someone Else

If you can explain a concept to a friend or your cat, you actually understand it. Feynman technique for the W.
