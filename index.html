Chapter 05 / Exam tactics

Tips

How to not lose free marks.

free marks alert

Exam survival basics

Learn sociologists properly. Use PEEL paragraphs. Nail definition questions. Self-mark. Manage your time. Use sociological terminology.

theory marks

Learn sociologists properly

Learn sociologists properly. Examiners love it when you use theorists correctly.

structure

PEEL paragraphs

Point, Evidence, Explanation, Link back to the question. This formula is your GOD. Variations like PEEEL and PEEAL are also good.

quick wins

Definition questions

Keep definition questions short, precise, and supported with an example.

feedback loop

Self-marking

Self-mark with the mark scheme open. It's the fastest way to learn how examiners think.

exam stamina

Time management

Practice past papers under timed conditions, get a smooth pen, and train your hand.

voice

Sociological terminology

Use specific sociological vocabulary that shows you know what you're talking about.

Chapter 06 / Real talk

Advice

Aka "what actually works."

Active recall

Highlighting your notes in many different colours and calling it "studying" is not studying. Test yourself. Close the book. Say it out loud.

Spaced repetition

Use spaced repetition. Review things just before you would forget them instead of cramming.

Past papers early

Do past papers early. The exam has a format, so get familiar with it before it surprises you.

Teach someone else

Teach someone else. If you can explain a concept clearly, you actually understand it. Feynman technique for the W.
