🧠 General Study Advice

aka “what actually works”

Active Recall

Highlighting notes in four colours and calling it studying is not studying. Close the book. Test yourself. Say it out loud.

Spaced Repetition

Review concepts before you forget them instead of cramming the night before. Anki is genuinely amazing for this.

Past Papers Early

Learn the exam format early. Understanding how to answer the questions is half the battle.

Teach Someone Else

If you can explain a concept to another person, you actually understand it. Feynman technique for the win.

💡 Sociology Exam Technique

aka “how to stop losing easy marks”

Learn Sociologists

Name-dropping theorists properly matters. “Durkheim argued that…” sounds far stronger than “a sociologist said…”.

PEEL Paragraphs

Point → Evidence → Explanation → Link. This structure is unbelievably reliable for extended answers.

Definition Questions

Short. Precise. One example. The examiner does not want your autobiography.

Mark Your Own Papers

Use the mark scheme every single time. It teaches you how examiners think.

Time Management

Practice under timed conditions. Also: get a smooth pen and train your hand stamina.

Sociological Vocabulary

Use subject-specific terminology consistently. It makes your answers sound far more analytical.
