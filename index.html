Study Tips

Expert advice
🎯 Key Concept

Focus on theories: Make sure you understand major sociological theories (functionalism, conflict theory, symbolic interactionism) before tackling specific topics.

📝 Essay Writing

Use examples: Always support your arguments with real-world studies and examples. Examiners love specific citations.

⏱️ Time Management

Practice timed essays: Familiarize yourself with exam timing. Most IGCSE Sociology papers have strict time limits.

🧠 Memory

Create flashcards: Key terms, theorists, and studies. Use spaced repetition for long-term retention.
